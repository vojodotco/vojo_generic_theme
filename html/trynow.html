Getting Started

Start sharing stories now

To create an account just call (617) 286-5071


  • Post your first story

    Call (617) 286-5071. Press 2 after the welcome message and leave an audio story. Hangup or press # to save the story.


  • Create your username and password

    After you post your first audio story, you’ll receive an SMS reply asking you to select a username. After entering your username, you will receive a confirmation with your new username and password.


  • Create and or join a group

    Now that you created an account, you can start posting stories! Feel free to create your own group or join an existing one. See the user manual for a list of configurable options for your group!
